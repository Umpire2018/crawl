'''2023年苏格兰民族党主席选举'''于2023年2月至3月间举行，目的是选出新任苏格兰民族党主席，以接替即将退休的[[妮古拉·斯特金]]。本次选举共3名候选人，于2月24日确定为凱特·福布斯、阿什·里根和洪姆扎·尤薩夫。选举于3月27日结束，由尤萨夫取得胜利，其在第一轮和第二轮选举中的得票率分别为 48.2%和52.1%，其中由于候选人里根中途退出，第二轮的得票率乃重新分配的结果。3月28日，尤萨夫当选[[蘇格蘭首席部長]]<ref>{{cite news |date=2011-05-06 |title=尤薩夫當選蘇格蘭民族黨黨魁成首名非白人蘇格蘭首席部長 |work=香港01 |url=https://www.hk01.com/ |access-date=2024-04-16 |archive-date=2019-03-30 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190330061157/https://www.hk01.com/ |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

此次选举是苏格兰民族党将近20年来第一次存在竞争的党魁选举，此前上一任主席[[妮古拉·斯特金]]在2014年的选举中是全票当选。选举期间，苏格兰议会媒体主管穆雷·富特和行政长官{{link-en|彼得·默雷尔|Peter Murrell}}（同时是妮古拉·斯特金的丈夫）由于被指虚报党员人数，先后于3月17日及18日辞职<ref name="Resignations" />。

==议题==
本次选举的议题主要集中在经济、苏格兰独立及性别认同法案改革等三个方面<ref name="partyssoul">{{Cite news |last=Cook |first=James |date=2023-03-13 |title=SNP leadership: The battle raging for the party's soul |work=BBC News |url=https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-scotland-scotland-politics-64939777 |accessdate=2024-04-20 |archive-date=2023-08-02 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230802025316/https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-scotland-scotland-politics-64939777 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

===经济===
选举前，民族党及其盟友绿党领导下的苏格兰政府所推行的禁止钻探新的石油及天然气资源、{{link-en|飲料瓶回收法|Container-deposit legislation|饮料瓶回收计划}}、限制酒精类广告等措施被部分批评人士认为阻碍了苏格兰的发展。同时左派党派批评政府在经济管理方面奉行[[新自由主義]]、抛售绿色能源及支持设立[[自由港]]<ref name=partyssoul />。

三名候选人中，凱特·福布斯被认为对商业领域最为友好。外界推测福布斯或阿什·里根中任何一人当选都会对民族党与绿党之间的分权协议构成威胁。三名候选人均表示不看好饮料瓶回收计划<ref name=partyssoul />。

=== 苏格兰独立 ===
寻求苏格兰独立是民族党的中心目标，因此这一议题也一直视作这次选举的主要议题。

三名候选人中，阿什·里根提出的方法最为激进<ref>{{Cite news |last=Carrell |first=Severin |date=2023-02-24 |title=Ash Regan outlines Scottish independence plan as SNP nominations close |language=en-GB |work=The Guardian |url=https://www.theguardian.com/politics/2023/feb/24/scottish-independence-ash-regan-campaign-launch-snp-first-minister |access-date=2024-04-16}}</ref>，她表示她会把未来所有包括苏格兰和整个英国在内的议会选举视为事实上的独立公投，主张只要超过50%的选民支持独立，便无需再举行公投，而是直接开启与英国政府的谈判程序。凱特·福布斯和洪姆扎·尤薩夫也将独立当作博取支持的重要筹码，其中福布斯承诺会在当选后三个月内推动公投，但未有说明具体策略<ref name=partyssoul />。同时三人均表示在下次英国大选举行之后的[[悬峙议会|议会悬峙]]阶段，将对支持[[工党 (英国)|工党]]持开放态度，以此来换取执行独立公投的权利。但工党领袖[[凱爾·斯塔摩]]回应称在任何情况下都不会同意这样的协议<ref name=partyssoul />。

==选举结果==
在本次选举中，洪姆扎·尤萨夫在第二轮中以52%的得票率胜出，凯特·福布斯居次。由于阿什·里根在第一轮被淘汰，故将其列为第二候选人的选票在第二轮被平均分配给尤萨夫和福布斯<ref name=":14">{{Cite news |date=2023-03-27 |title=Humza Yousaf elected leader of Scottish National party |language=en-GB |work=The Guardian |last=Brooks |first=Libby |url=https://www.theguardian.com/politics/2023/mar/27/humza-yousaf-elected-leader-scottish-national-party-snp |access-date=2024-04-20 |issn=0261-3077 |archive-date=2023-06-02 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230602085923/https://www.theguardian.com/politics/2023/mar/27/humza-yousaf-elected-leader-scottish-national-party-snp |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref name=":15">{{Cite news |date=2023-03-27 |title=Humza Yousaf to succeed Sturgeon as SNP leader |language=en-GB |work=BBC News |url=https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-scotland-scotland-politics-65086551 |access-date=2024-04-20 |archive-date=2023-03-27 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230327131327/https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-scotland-scotland-politics-65086551 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

此次选举一共派发了50,494张选票，与民族党总人数72,169人相比，参选率69.97%。在所有选票中，共有48,645张电子票和1,849张实体票，实体票中有3张被拒收<ref name=":14" /><ref name=":15" />

==后续==
[[File:New Cabinet unveiled.jpg|thumb|220x220px|尤萨夫的内阁成员，其中没有任何福布斯的支持者]]
本次选举被BBC等一些媒体视为民族党内部已出现严重分裂的一个见证<ref>{{cite news |date=2023-03-27 |title=https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-scotland-scotland-politics-65086551 |url=https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-scotland-scotland-politics-65086551 |access-date=2024-04-20 |website=BBC News |language=en-gb |archive-date=2023-03-27 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230327131327/https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-scotland-scotland-politics-65086551 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref name=":14" />。BBC将尤萨夫的胜利描述为“微弱优势当选”（razor-thin）<ref>{{cite news |last=Hastie |first=Paul |date=2023-03-27 |title=Humza Yousaf announced as new SNP leader |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/live/uk-scotland-65086830 |access-date=2024-04-20 |website=BBC News |language=en-gb |archive-date=2024-05-09 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240509074252/https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/live/uk-scotland-65086830 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。尤萨夫在胜选后的演讲中也强调了民族党需要团结的问题<ref>{{cite news |title=Humza Yousaf elected new SNP leader beating Kate Forbes by 52% to 48% on second preferences – UK politics live |date=27 March 2023 |language=en-GB |work=The Guardian |url=https://www.theguardian.com/politics/live/2023/mar/27/labour-rishi-sunak-anti-social-behaviour-snp-scotland-humza-yousaf-kate-forbes-ash-regan-uk-illegal-migration-politics-live |access-date=2024-04-20 |issn=0261-3077}}</ref> 。

选举结束当天，[[苏格兰绿党|绿党]]方面表示支持尤萨夫成为新一任首席部长<ref>{{Cite web |date=2023-03-27 |title=Scottish Greens unanimously back Humza Yousaf for first minister |url=https://www.holyrood.com/news/view,scottish-greens-unanimously-back-humza-yousaf-for-first-minister |access-date=2024-04-20 |website=Holyrood Website |language=en |archive-date=2024-01-18 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240118070236/https://www.holyrood.com/news/view,scottish-greens-unanimously-back-humza-yousaf-for-first-minister |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref>{{Cite news |date=2023-03-27 |title=Nicola Sturgeon formally resigns as first minister |language=en-GB |work=BBC News |url=https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-scotland-scotland-politics-65093281 |access-date=2024-04-20 |archive-date=2024-04-29 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240429080027/https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-scotland-scotland-politics-65093281 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。3月28日，即选举结束的第二天，尤萨夫正式当选首席部长，成为自该职位于1999年设立以来，第一位当选的有色人种和穆斯林<ref>{{Cite news |date=2023-03-28 |title=Humza Yousaf confirmed as Scotland's new first minister |language=en-GB |work=BBC News |url=https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-scotland-scotland-politics-65098609 |access-date=2024-04-20 |archive-date=2023-03-29 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230329042141/https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-scotland-scotland-politics-65098609 |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref>{{Cite news |date=2023-03-28 |title=New SNP leader Humza Yousaf voted in as Scotland's first minister |language=en-GB |work=The Guardian |url=https://www.theguardian.com/uk-news/2023/mar/28/new-snp-leader-humza-yousaf-voted-in-scotland-first-minister |access-date=2024-04-20 |issn=0261-3077}}</ref>。

尤萨夫在当选后曾邀请福布斯进入内阁，并愿意将农村事务交给后者管理，但遭到后者拒绝。福布斯更于议会宣布尤萨夫将就任新一任首席部长的几小时后宣布离开政府，但她后来亦在Twitter上表示相信尤萨夫能够胜任<ref>{{Cite news |date=2023-03-28 |title=Kate Forbes: SNP leadership candidate leaves government |language=en-GB |work=BBC News |url=https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-scotland-scotland-politics-65105951 |access-date=2024-04-20 |archive-date=2024-04-29 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240429080026/https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-scotland-scotland-politics-65105951 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

尤萨夫在任命政府官员时，没有指定任何之前支持福布斯的人士<ref>{{Cite web |title=Humza Yousaf can't brag about a government of all talents when opponents brand him 'Humza Useless' |url=https://news.sky.com/story/precedent-suggests-winner-takes-all-interpretation-of-humza-yousafs-52-48-victory-will-only-be-start-of-difficulties-12845848 |access-date=2023-03-31 |website=Sky News |language=en |archive-date=2023-05-29 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230529042711/https://news.sky.com/story/precedent-suggests-winner-takes-all-interpretation-of-humza-yousafs-52-48-victory-will-only-be-start-of-difficulties-12845848 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

2023年10月，选举候选人之一里根加入阿尔巴党，她表示离开民族党的原因是她对民族党领导层已经不抱希望以及感觉到民族党对独立的追求已经减弱<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-scotland-67248844 |title=Former SNP leadership candidate Ash Regan defects to Alba party |date=2023-10-28 |accessdate=2024-04-20 |work=BBC News |publisher=BBC |archive-date=2024-04-28 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240428074620/https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-scotland-67248844 |dead-url=no }}</ref>。
