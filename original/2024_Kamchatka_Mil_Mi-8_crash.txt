On 31 August 2024, a [[Mil Mi-8]] helicopter operating a scheduled charter flight crashed in [[Kamchatka Krai]], Russia, killing all 22 people on board. The aircraft, which was operated by Vityaz-Aero on a sightseeing tour over the {{Ill|Vachkazhets|ru|Вачкажец}} volcano, crashed in poor weather shortly after take-off.
