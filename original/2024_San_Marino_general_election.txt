General elections were held in [[San Marino]] on 9 June 2024.<ref>[https://www.elezioni.sm/on-line/home/articolo26016504.html Elezioni Politiche del 9 giugno 2024 - Prime scadenze e primi adempimenti]</ref>

==Electoral system==
{{Main|Elections in San Marino}}
The 60 members of the [[Grand and General Council]] are elected by [[proportional representation]], with seats allocated using the [[d'Hondt method]]. The [[electoral threshold]] is calculated by multiplying the number of parties running in the elections by 0.4, with a maximum possible threshold of 3.5%.<ref>[http://www.ipu.org/parline-e/reports/2273_B.htm Electoral system] IPU</ref>

If no party receives a majority, or the two largest parties are unable to form a coalition government within thirty days of the elections, a runoff election will be held between the two most popular coalitions, with the winner receiving a [[Majority jackpot system|majority jackpot]] to give them a majority.<ref>[http://www.elezioni.sm/on-line/home/elezioni-politiche/sistema-elettorale.html Electoral system in force in the Republic of San Marino] Secretariat of State for Internal Affairs {{in lang|it}}</ref><ref name="DD">[https://web.archive.org/web/20200913011105/https://sudd.ch/event.php?lang=fr&id=sm012019 San Marino, 2. Juni 2019 : Zweiter Wahlgang bei Parlamentswahlen] Direct Democracy</ref>

==Campaign==
A political collaboration between [[Future Republic]] (RF) and Area Democratica was announced on 18 April 2024. Area Democratica nominated some of its representatives on the RF list as independent candidates for the election.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Fiocca |first=Alessandro |date=2024-04-19 |title=San Marino. Repubblica Futura e Area Democratica in lista insieme alle prossime elezioni |url=https://www.libertas.sm/san-marino-repubblica-futura-e-area-democratica-in-lista-insieme-alle-prossime-elezioni/ |access-date=2024-04-19 |website=Libertas |language=it-IT}}</ref>

An electoral alliance was formed for the elections: 

* '''Reformist Alliance''' (''Alleanza Riformista''), an alliance of Movimento Ideali Socialisti, [[We Sammarineses]], and Ēlego.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Fiocca |first=Alessandro |date=2024-04-17 |title=San Marino. Alleanza Riformista accoglie Elego e conferma la coalizione con la DC: "Progetto ambizioso e pragmatico" |url=https://www.libertas.sm/san-marino-la-lista-con-mis-noi-sammarinesi-elego-e-socialisti-di-alleanza-riformista-confermano-la-coalizione-con-la-dc/ |access-date=2024-04-19 |website=Libertas |language=it-IT}}</ref>

On 18 April, a coalition was formed between the [[Sammarinese Christian Democratic Party]] and the Reformist Alliance,<ref>{{Cite web |last=Lattanzi |first=Andrea |date=2024-04-18 |title=Verso le elezioni politiche a San Marino, Alleanza riformista: "Un nuovo capitolo per la politica sammarinese" |url=https://www.libertas.sm/verso-le-elezioni-politiche-a-san-marino-alleanza-riformista-un-nuovo-capitolo-per-la-politica-sammarinese/ |access-date=2024-04-19 |website=Libertas |language=it-IT}}</ref> known as [[Democracy and Freedom (San Marino)|Democracy and Freedom]].<ref>https://www.libertas.sm/san-marino-coalizione-dc-e-ar-democrazia-e-liberta/</ref>

On 24 April an coalition of the [[Party of Socialists and Democrats]], [[Libera San Marino]] and the [[Socialist Party (San Marino)|Socialist Party]] was launched.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.libertas.sm/elezioni-a-san-marino-la-coalizione-libera-ps-psd-si-presenta-video/ | title=Elezioni a San Marino, la coalizione Libera-Ps-PSD si presenta - VIDEO | date=24 April 2024 }}</ref>
