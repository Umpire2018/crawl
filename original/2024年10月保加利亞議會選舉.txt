{{保加利亞政治}}

'''2024年10月保加利亞議會選舉'''是[[保加利亞]]於2024年10月27日舉行的[[国民议会 (保加利亚)|國民議會]]提前選舉，<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.reuters.com/world/europe/bulgaria-hold-another-snap-parliamentary-election-oct-27-says-president-2024-08-26/|title=Bulgaria to hold another snap parliamentary election on Oct. 27, says president|website=Reuters|date=2024-08-26}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |title=Central Election Commission Adopts Schedule for October 27 Snap Parliamentary Elections |url=https://www.bta.bg/en/news/bulgaria/732377-central-election-commission-adopts-schedule-for-october-27-snap-parliamentary-el |access-date=2024-08-27 |website=www.bta.bg |language=en |archive-date=2024-11-11 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20241111142618/https://www.bta.bg/en/news/bulgaria/732377-central-election-commission-adopts-schedule-for-october-27-snap-parliamentary-el |dead-url=no }}</ref>原因是[[2024年6月保加利亞議會選舉|2024年6月]]的國會選舉後，三次組閣全部失敗，這是該國自2021年以來第六次提前選舉。

==選舉制度==
國民議會的240名議員是透過[[開放名單]]和[[比例代表制]]從31個[[複數選區制|複數選區]]（席位数從4到19席不等）中選出。所有政黨或選舉聯盟的[[選舉門檻]]均為4%，席次依[[黑爾數額]][[最大餘額法]]分配。<ref>{{Cite web |title=Republic of Bulgaria Election for Narodno Sabranie (Bulgarian National Assembly) |url=https://www.electionguide.org/elections/id/3595/ |website=IFES Election Guide |access-date=2024-11-10 |archive-date=2021-05-04 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210504022432/https://www.electionguide.org/elections/id/3595/ |dead-url=no }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Electoral system for national legislature – Bulgaria|url=https://www.idea.int/answer/ans1303551645461599|website=International IDEA|access-date=2024-11-10|archive-date=2021-07-07|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210707030119/https://www.idea.int/answer/ans1303551645461599|dead-url=no}}</ref>
