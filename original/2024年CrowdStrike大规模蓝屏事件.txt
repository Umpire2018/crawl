==事件背景==
[[File:CrowdStrike logo.svg|thumb|CrowdStrike商标（迄2024年）]]

[[CrowdStrike]]提供一系列安全软件保护[[电子计算机|计算机]]免于[[网络攻击]]。旗下[[漏洞扫描器]]“猎鹰传感器”（Falcon Sensor）產品在个人电脑[[操作系统]]的[[内核]]层面安装[[端點偵測與回應]]传感器，以检测和预防威胁。CrowdStrike会定期向客户分发[[修補程式]]，使他们的计算机能够应对新的威胁<ref name="the-register-workaround">{{cite web |last1=Sharwood |first1=Simon |title=CrowdStrike code update bricking Windows machines around the world |url=https://www.theregister.com/2024/07/19/crowdstrike_falcon_sensor_bsod_incident/ |access-date=2024-07-19 |website=The Register |archive-date=2024-07-19 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240719143002/https://www.theregister.com/2024/07/19/crowdstrike_falcon_sensor_bsod_incident/ |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

2009年，微软与[[欧盟]]达成协议，要求微软必须向第三方安全软件开发商开放相关[[应用程序接口]]（API）。因此，包括CrowdStrike在内的安全软件均拥有系统内核级别的访问[[特权 (Windows)|权限]]。<ref>{{Cite web|title=微软透露欧盟限制导致无法锁定Windows 这也是CrowdStrike蓝屏事件的原因之一|url=https://www.landiannews.com/archives/105068.html|website=蓝点网|date=2024-07-22|language=zh-Hans-CN|access-date=2024-07-23|archive-date=2024-07-26|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240726185254/https://www.landiannews.com/archives/105068.html|dead-url=no}}</ref>

2024年7月18日，即在此次问题更新前，[[Microsoft Azure]]云服务发生异常，导致美国中部部分Azure用户无法访问其云存储及[[Microsoft 365]]服务<ref>{{cite news | url = https://www.techradar.com/pro/microsoft-says-its-cloud-services-are-back-up-after-major-outage | title = Microsoft says its cloud services are back up after major outage | date = 2024-07-19 | work = {{tsl|en|TechRadar||TechRadar}} | accessdate = 2024-07-20 | archive-date = 2024-07-25 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20240725184140/https://www.techradar.com/pro/microsoft-says-its-cloud-services-are-back-up-after-major-outage | dead-url = no }}</ref>。微软表示，两起事件并无关系，但对这些受影响公司的客户来说，问题却更加复杂<ref>{{cite news | url = https://www.nytimes.com/2024/07/19/business/microsoft-outage-cause-azure-crowdstrike.html | title = What Caused Such a Widespread Tech Meltdown? | date = 2024-07-19 | accessdate = 2024-07-19 | work = [[纽约时报|The New York Times]] | archive-date = 2024-07-19 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20240719102658/https://www.nytimes.com/2024/07/19/business/microsoft-outage-cause-azure-crowdstrike.html | dead-url = no }}</ref>。

[[协调世界时]]（UTC）7月19日早上4时09分，部署在Azure的Windows[[虛擬機器]]开始重启及崩溃<ref>{{Cite web |title=Azure status |url=https://azure.status.microsoft/en-us/status/ |access-date=2024-07-19 |website=azure.status.microsoft |archive-date=2024-07-25 |archive-url=https://archive.today/20240725005215/https://azure.status.microsoft/en-us/status/ |dead-url=no }}</ref>，6时48分，[[Google计算引擎]]报告此问题。7时15分，[[Google]]宣布CrowdStrike更新存在问题<ref>{{Cite web |title=Google Cloud Service Health |url=https://status.cloud.google.com/incidents/DK3LfKowzJPpZq4Q9YqP |access-date=2024-07-19 |website=status.cloud.google.com |archive-date=2024-07-19 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240719163245/https://status.cloud.google.com/incidents/DK3LfKowzJPpZq4Q9YqP |dead-url=no }}</ref>。

CrowdStrike首席执行官{{Link-en|乔治·库尔茨|George Kurtz}}确定此事由CrowdStrike的异常驱动更新造成，而非网络攻击<ref name="Browne-240719" /><ref name="NDTV.com-240719" />。
