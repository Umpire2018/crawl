'''2024年亚拉巴马州联邦众议员选举'''于11月5日举行，选举产生[[亚拉巴马州]]的七名[[联邦众议员]]，其中[[阿拉巴馬州國會選區|每个选区]]各选出一名。此次选举与2024年美国总统选举同时进行，与之同时举行的还包括联邦参议院选举、其他州和地方选举，以及其他地区的联邦众议员选举。

主要政党候选人的资格审查于2023年11月10日结束。初选于2024年3月5日举行，因为亚拉巴马州是总统选举日程中的[[超級星期二|超级星期二]]州。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://altoday.com/archives/53817-gop-candidate-qualifying-opens-on-october-16|title=GOP candidate qualifying opens on October 16|last=Moseley|first=Brandon|work=Alabama Today|date=2023-09-12|access-date=2023-11-06|archive-date=2023-11-06|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20231106200454/https://altoday.com/archives/53817-gop-candidate-qualifying-opens-on-october-16|dead-url=no}}</ref>在初选中如果没有候选人获得超过50%的票数，将举行第二轮初选。第二轮初选定于2024年4月16日举行<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.wvtm13.com/article/election-day-2024-alabama-primary/45769269|title=Alabama 2024 Primary Election: Races, registration and important dates|last=Reitz|first=Ian|work=WVTM-13|date=2023-11-07|access-date=2023-11-10|archive-date=2024-02-26|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240226182732/https://www.wvtm13.com/article/election-day-2024-alabama-primary/45769269|dead-url=no}}</ref>，这在亚拉巴马州第二国会选区的民主党和共和党初选中都有发生。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.al.com/news/2024/03/brewbaker-advances-to-runoff-in-gop-race-in-alabamas-new-2nd-district.html|title=Brewbaker advances to runoff in GOP race in Alabama's new 2nd District|last=Cason|first=Mike|work=AL.com|date=2024-03-05|access-date=2024-03-05|archive-date=2024-08-25|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240825201936/https://www.al.com/news/2024/03/brewbaker-advances-to-runoff-in-gop-race-in-alabamas-new-2nd-district.html|dead-url=no}}</ref>决选将于2024年11月5日举行。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.wvtm13.com/article/black-friday-christmas-tree-sale/38301932|title=Alabama 2024 primary: Here are the Democratic and Republican Congressional candidates who have qualified|last=Reitz|first=Ian|work=WVTM-13|date=2023-11-03|access-date=2023-11-06}}</ref>

=== 重新划分选区流程 ===
2023年7月，[[亚拉巴马州议会]]召开特别会议，以批准新的国会选区地图，最终期限为7月21日。[[亚拉巴马州众议院]]和[[亚拉巴马州参议院|参议院]]分别通过了两份不同的选区地图，但两者均未包含第二个黑人占多数的选区。亚拉巴马州众议院通过了一份由共和党议员克里斯·普林格尔提出的地图，该地图将亚拉巴马州第二国会选区的黑人适龄选民人口比例提高至42%，但仍未达到黑人多数选区的标准。亚拉巴马州参议院则通过了一份由共和党参议员史蒂夫·利文斯顿提出的地图，将该比例提高至38%。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.alreporter.com/2023/07/20/house-senate-pass-separate-congressional-maps-but-anticipate-a-compromise/|title=House, Senate pass separate congressional maps but anticipate a compromise|last=Holmes|first=Jacob|work=Alabama Political Reporter|date=2023-07-20|access-date=2023-07-20|archive-date=2024-09-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240917152732/https://www.alreporter.com/2023/07/20/house-senate-pass-separate-congressional-maps-but-anticipate-a-compromise/|dead-url=no}}</ref>随后，亚拉巴马州立法议会两党成员组成的特别协商委员会对地图进行了修改，并于7月21日通过了新的版本。<ref>{{Cite name="1819b">{{Cite web|url=https://1819news.com/news/item/new-congressional-redistricting-map-for-alabama-signed-into-law|title=New congressional redistricting map for Alabama signed into === 最终确定的地图 ===
在亚拉巴马州总检察长史蒂夫·马歇尔的领导下，亚拉巴马州请求暂停联邦法庭关于不通过议会新地图的裁定。2023年9月26日，美国最高法院再次拒绝了亚拉巴马州的请求，这意味着特别负责人绘制的选区地图将在2024年选举中使用。所有三份提出的补救性地图都将第二国会选区的黑人适龄选民人口比例提高到48.5%至50.1%之间。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://1819news.com/news/item/scotus-denies-alabamas-request-for-stay-on-federal-courts-redistricting-ruling|title=SCOTUS denies Alabama's request for stay on federal court's redistricting ruling|last=Taylor|first=Caleb|work=1819 News|date=2023-09-26|access-date=2023-09-26}}</ref>与此同时，亚拉巴马州民主党黑人议员团体亚拉巴马州民主党大会也在同一天宣布计划对特别负责人绘制的地图提出异议，称这些地图未能充分划定一个黑人多数选区。民主党大会提出了自己的一份重新划定选区的地图作为异议内容<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.al.com/politics/2023/09/alabama-democratic-conference-to-file-objection-to-special-master-offer-own-redistricting-map.html|title=Alabama Democratic Conference to file objection to special master, offer own redistricting map|last=Koplowitz|first=Howard|work=AL.com|date=2023-09-26|access-date=2023-09-26|archive-date=2024-07-10|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240710185505/https://www.al.com/politics/2023/09/alabama-democratic-conference-to-file-objection-to-special-master-offer-own-redistricting-map.html|dead-url=no}}</ref>，但该地图被联邦法庭否决。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://alabamareflector.com/briefs/federal-court-denies-intervention-by-joe-reed-led-group-in-alabama-redistricting-case/|title=Federal court denies intervention by Joe Reed-led group in Alabama redistricting case|last=Stephenson|first=Jemma|work=Alabama Reflector|date=2023-07-26|access-date=2024-03-05|archive-date=2024-11-08|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20241108035319/https://alabamareflector.com/briefs/federal-court-denies-intervention-by-joe-reed-led-group-in-alabama-redistricting-case/|dead-url=no}}</ref>

在该案的另一次听证会后，联邦法庭于2023年10月5日发布意见，要求亚拉巴马州实施由特别负责人绘制的国会选区地图——补救计划3。亚拉巴马州州务卿韦斯·艾伦表示，州务卿办公室将按照新的地图进行2024年选举的安排。在补救计划3中，亚拉巴马州第二国会选区的黑人适龄选民人口比例为48.7%，且在17次模拟选举中，黑人倾向候选人在其中16次中获胜。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://alabamareflector.com/2023/10/05/federal-court-selects-new-alabama-congressional-map/|title=Federal court selects new Alabama congressional map|last1=Lyman|first1=Brian|last2=Stephenson|first2=Jemma|work=Alabama Reflector|date=2023-10-05|access-date=2023-10-05|archive-date=2024-11-07|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20241107231959/https://alabamareflector.com/2023/10/05/federal-court-selects-new-alabama-congressional-map/|dead-url=no}}</ref>

== 概述 ==
共和党赢得了其中五个选区<ref name="politico_alabama">{{cite web |title=Alabama House election results |url=https://www.politico.com/2024-election/results/alabama/house/ |website=Politico |access-date=2024-11-22 |archive-date=2024-03-05 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240305193756/https://www.politico.com/2024-election/results/alabama/house/ |dead-url=no }}</ref>，民主党赢得了两个选区即新划定<ref name="al_02_guardian">{{cite news |last1=Walker |first1=Adria |title=‘Excitement in the air’: newly created Alabama district votes for first time |url=https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2024/nov/05/new-alabama-congressional-district-two |access-date=2024-11-22 |agency=The Guardian |date=2024-11-05}}</ref>的第二选区和现有的第七选区。这两个选区的黑人选民占多数，而共和党赢得的选区则主要由白人选民构成。<ref>{{cite news |last1=Walker |first1=Chris |title=Shomari Figures Wins in District Where Alabama GOP Tried to Dilute Black Vote |url=https://truthout.org/articles/shomari-figures-wins-in-district-where-alabama-gop-tried-to-dilute-black-vote/ |access-date=2024-11-22 |agency=truthout |date=2024-11-07}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Who Will Represent Alabama’s New Democratic District? |url=https://prospect.org/politics/2024-01-29-alabamas-new-democratic-district/ |access-date=2024-11-22 |agency=The American Prospect |date=2024-01-29 |archive-date=2024-10-11 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20241011092317/https://prospect.org/politics/2024-01-29-alabamas-new-democratic-district/ |dead-url=no }}</ref>这一结果与亚拉巴马州黑人选民倾向支持民主党的投票趋势一致。<ref>{{cite news |title=Surprise Supreme Court win for Black voters could give Democrats an electoral boost |url=https://www.nbcnews.com/politics/2024-election/surprise-supreme-court-win-black-voters-give-democrats-electoral-boost-rcna88358 |access-date=2024-11-22 |agency=NBC News |date=2023-06-09}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last1=Willis |first1=Alexander |title=Alabama GOP makes ‘historic’ gains with minority voters in 2024 election |url=https://aldailynews.com/alabama-gop-makes-historic-gains-with-minority-voters-in-2024-election/ |access-date=2024-11-22 |agency=Alabama Daily News |date=2024-11-22}}</ref>民主党在七个选区中的四个选区提名了候选人，而共和党则在第三、第四和第五选区几乎毫无挑战地获胜。除了这几个选区之外，共和党在第一选区取得了最好的成绩，赢得了78.4%的选票。<ref>{{cite web |title=Alabama House Results 2024 |url=https://www.nbcnews.com/politics/2024-elections/alabama-house-results |website=NBC News |access-date=2024-11-22}}</ref>

共和党在2024年的选举中取得了比2022年更好的成绩，获得了多出3.5%的选票。<ref>{{cite web |title=Alabama U.S. House Election Results 2022 |url=https://www.nbcnews.com/politics/2022-elections/alabama-house-results |website=NBC News |access-date=2024-11-24 |archive-date=2024-11-08 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20241108144858/https://www.nbcnews.com/politics/2022-elections/alabama-house-results |dead-url=no }}</ref>然而这次选举也是自2008年以来，民主党首次在亚拉巴马州赢得超过一个席位。<ref>{{cite web |title=Alabama Presidential Election Voting History |url=https://www.270towin.com/states/Alabama |website=270 to Win |access-date=2024-11-24 |archive-date=2020-11-12 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201112201233/https://www.270towin.com/states/Alabama |dead-url=no }}</ref>此外这也是亚拉巴马州首次同时选出两位黑人众议员。<ref>{{cite news |last1=Stephenson |first1=Jemma |title=For 1st time in history, Alabama will have two Black U.S. House members serving together |url=https://alabamareflector.com/2024/11/11/alabama-will-have-two-black-u-s-house-members-serving-together-for-1st-time-in-history/ |access-date=2024-11-24 |agency=Alabama Reflector |date=2024-11-11}}</ref>

第三选区位于亚拉巴马州东部，涵盖了[[卡爾霍恩縣 (阿拉巴馬州)|卡尔洪县]]、[[埃托瓦县]]、[[李郡 (阿拉巴馬州)|李县]]和[[塔拉迪加县 (亚拉巴马州)|塔拉迪加县]]这四个县，主要城市包括[[安尼斯敦 (阿拉巴马州)|安尼斯顿]]、[[奥本 (亚拉巴马州)|奥本]]、[[加兹登 (亚拉巴马州)|加兹登]]和[[塔拉迪加 (阿拉巴马州)|塔拉迪加]]。现任议员是共和党人[[邁克·羅傑斯 (阿拉巴馬州政治人物)|迈克·罗杰斯]]，他在2022年以71.3%的得票率成功连任。<ref name="HPVT"/>

罗杰斯已宣布竞选连任。他因最初拒绝支持[[吉姆·喬丹 (美國政治人物)|吉姆·乔丹]]竞选[[2023年10月美國眾議院議長選舉|2023年10月]]的众议院议长，并表示愿意与[[哈基姆·杰弗里斯]]妥协支持其他候选人，而受到保守派的批评。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://1819news.com/news/item/rogers-draws-rebuke-from-conservatives-after-signaling-willingness-to-cut-deal-with-dems-to-resolve-u-s-house-speaker-impasse|title=Rogers draws rebuke from conservatives after signaling willingness to cut deal with Dems to resolve U.S. House Speaker impasse|last=Poor|first=Jeff|work=1819 News|date=2023-10-15|access-date=2023-10-16|archive-date=2024-06-20|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240620024644/https://1819news.com/news/item/rogers-draws-rebuke-from-conservatives-after-signaling-willingness-to-cut-deal-with-dems-to-resolve-u-s-house-speaker-impasse|dead-url=no}}</ref>对此，包括第三选区在内的亚拉巴马州共和党州执行委员会成员威胁要在此次选举中挑战他。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://1819news.com/news/item/algop-executive-committee-member-threatens-republican-primary-ballot-access-challenge-against-u-s-rep-rogers-over-house-speaker-saga|title=ALGOP executive committee member threatens Republican primary ballot access challenge against U.S. Rep. 尽管阿德霍尔特在初选中面临失败的竞争对手贾斯廷·霍尔库姆，但该选区没有民主党候选人获得参选资格。<ref name="WhoALNov" />

{{see also|亚拉巴马州第六国会选区}}
第六选区包括大伯明翰附近的州中部地区，其中包括[[伯明翰 (阿拉巴马州)|伯明翰市]]和[[傑佛遜縣 (阿拉巴馬州)|杰斐逊县]]的东北部以及周边郊区，囊括了[[比伯县 (亚拉巴马州)|比伯县]]、[[奇尔顿县]]、[[库萨县]]和[[謝爾比縣 (阿拉巴馬州)|谢尔比县]]。其他城市还包括[[阿拉巴斯特 (亚拉巴马州)|阿拉巴斯特]]、[[胡佛 (阿拉巴马州)|胡佛]]和[[蒙特瓦洛 (阿拉巴马州)|蒙特瓦洛]]。现任议员是共和党人[[加里·帕爾默 (政治人物)|加里·帕尔默]]，他在2022年以84.7%的得票率击败[[自由意志党 (美国)|自由意志党]]候选人后再次当选。<ref name="HPVT"/>

帕尔默的连任竞选引起了关注，因为他在2014年签署了美国任期限制承诺书，并承诺连任不超过五次。然而按照他之前的声明，他本应在2024年退休，但他仍决定争取第六个任期。<ref name="ALTPalmer">{{Cite web|url=https://altoday.com/archives/50389-gary-palmer-breaking-key-campaign-promise-after-praying-to-god-for-clarity|title=Gary Palmer breaking key campaign promise after praying to God for 'clarity'|last=Moseley|first=Brandon|work=Alabama Today|date=2023-03-11|access-date=2023-03-11|archive-date=2024-05-15|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240515143253/https://altoday.com/archives/50389-gary-palmer-breaking-key-campaign-promise-after-praying-to-god-for-clarity|dead-url=no}}</ref>帕尔默解释了连任的理由，其中包括亚拉巴马州国会议员的频繁更替、他在众议院共和党内的领导地位上升，<ref name="WilkinsCohen">{{Cite web|url=https://about.bgov.com/news/gop-maps-out-next-agenda-with-aid-of-quiet-lawmaker-from-alabama/|title=GOP Maps Out Next Agenda With Aid of Quiet Lawmaker From Alabama|last1=Wilkins|first1=Emily|last2=Cohen|first2=Zach|work=Bloomberg Government|date=2022-03-04|access-date=2022-12-12|archive-date=2024-07-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240717102820/https://about.bgov.com/news/gop-maps-out-next-agenda-with-aid-of-quiet-lawmaker-from-alabama/|dead-url=no}}</ref>以及个人祷告，他表示自己“祈求上帝为我指引”。<ref name="ALTPalmer" />他对媒体报道（包括AL.com的一篇文章）提出异议，认为这些报道错误地将他的五任期承诺与美国任期限制承诺联系起来，实际上该承诺仅适用于提案立法。不过帕尔默承认在2014年竞选时确实承诺过不超过五个任期，并表示他会“承担”违背该承诺的责任。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://1819news.com/news/item/u-s-rep-palmer-running-again-in-2024-claims-pledge-was-to-support-term-limit-legislation-but-acknowledges-saying-would-only-serve-five-terms|title=U.S. Rep. Palmer running again in 2024; Claims pledge was to support term-limit legislation, but acknowledges saying would only serve five terms|last=Blakely|first=Will|work=1819 News|date=2023-03-14|access-date=2023-03-14|archive-date=2024-03-04|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240304215630/https://1819news.com/news/item/u-s-rep-palmer-running-again-in-2024-claims-pledge-was-to-support-term-limit-legislation-but-acknowledges-saying-would-only-serve-five-terms|dead-url=no}}</ref>

{{see also|亚拉巴马州第六国会选区}}
第七选区位于亚拉巴马州西中部的“黑带”地区，涵盖了[[迪莫波利斯]]市、[[格林斯博罗 (阿拉巴马州)|格林斯博罗]]市和[[塞尔玛 (阿拉巴马州)|塞尔玛]]市，并包括了[[伯明翰 (阿拉巴马州)|伯明翰市]]和[[塔斯卡卢萨]]市中的黑人多数区域。现任议员是民主党人[[特丽·塞维尔]]，她在2022年以63.6%的得票率成功连任。<ref name="HPVT"/>

[[特丽·塞维尔]]正在竞选连任第八个任期。<ref name="Sewell">{{Cite web|url=https://docquery.fec.gov/cgi-bin/forms/H0AL07086/1675615/|title=Terri Sewell FEC Statement of Candidacy|access-date=2023-03-07|archive-date=2023-08-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230816191521/https://docquery.fec.gov/cgi-bin/forms/H0AL07086/1675615/|dead-url=no}}</ref><ref name="Sewell2024">{{cite news |last1=Lesniewski |first1=Niels |last2=McIntire |first2=Mary Ellen |title=Alabama ruling could help Democrats in multiple states |url=https://rollcall.com/2023/06/08/alabama-ruling-could-help-democrats-in-multiple-states/ |work=Roll Call |date=2023-06-08 |quote=Sewell, who lives in Birmingham, said she expects to run again in the 7th District, but acknowledged that her district could change under a new map.}}</ref>州参议员兼参议院少数党领袖鲍比·辛格尔顿曾成立筹备委员会，计划挑战塞维尔<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://aldailynews.com/singleton-considering-congressional-run-against-sewell/|title=Singleton considering congressional run against Sewell|work=Alabama Daily News|date=2023-09-26|access-date=2023-11-12|archive-date=2023-12-08|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20231208114412/https://aldailynews.com/singleton-considering-congressional-run-against-sewell/|dead-url=no}}</ref>，但最终未参选。然而，塞维尔在民主党初选中还是遇到了一位未成功的挑战者——克里斯·戴维斯。<ref name="WhoALNov" />

在共和党初选中，候选人克里斯蒂安·霍恩于2024年2月25日正式宣布退出竞选，留下罗宾·利塔克这一唯一活跃的候选人争夺共和党提名。《1819新闻》报道称，霍恩的选票将不予认证，亚拉巴马州共和党已收到霍恩退出竞选的通知。<ref name="horn1" />然而尽管霍恩已退出，他仍在3月5日的初选中获得了胜利。<ref name="horn2">{{Cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/interactive/2024/03/05/us/elections/results-alabama-us-house-7-primary.html|title=Alabama 7th Congressional District Primary Election Results|work=[[The New York Times]]|date=2024-03-05|access-date=2024-03-05|archive-date=2024-09-25|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240925172753/https://www.nytimes.com/interactive/2024/03/05/us/elections/results-alabama-us-house-7-primary.html|dead-url=no}}</ref>随后亚拉巴马州共和党发布声明，明确表示霍恩已正式退选，利塔克将成为党内2024年的提名候选人。<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.demopolistimes.com/2024/03/06/despite-winning-primary-horn-will-not-be-republican-nominee-after-being-allowed-to-withdraw-from-race-litaker-will-face-sewell-in-general-election/|title=Despite winning primary, Horn will not be Republican nominee after being allowed to withdraw from race. Litaker will face Sewell in general election.|work=The Demopolis Times|date=2024-03-06|access-date=2024-03-08}}</ref>
