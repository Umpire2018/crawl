{{Politics of Greece}}
[[Indirect election|Indirect]] '''presidential elections''' were held in [[Greece]] beginning on 25 January 2025 for the [[President of Greece|president of the Hellenic Republic]].<ref>{{cite news |title=The presidential election and latent risks |url=https://www.ekathimerini.com/opinion/1255954/the-presidential-election-and-latent-risks/ |access-date=16 December 2024 |work=eKathimerini |date=12 December 2024}}</ref> A second round was held on 31 January 2025 after no candidate won a majority in the [[Hellenic Parliament]], with the same result as in the first. A third round of voting was held on 6 February 2025, where no candidate reached the reduced required majority of 180. The fourth round was held on 12 February 2025, where [[Konstantinos Tasoulas]] was elected as President of Greece.

==Constitutional provisions==
According to Article 32 of the [[Constitution of Greece]], the president is elected for a five-year term by the [[Hellenic Parliament]] in a special session at least a month before the incumbent's term expires. The first and second rounds require a [[supermajority]] of 200 out of the 300-strong body, dropping to 180 on the third.<ref name=system/>

As of November 2019, after a constitutional amendment, Article 32, Paragraph 4 of the Greek Constitution which states about the election of the President was changed and the new reform of the paragraph is as follows: If after 3 ballots, the president is not elected, the 4th ballot's threshold will drop to 151 votes, and the 5th and last ballot would be contested between the two candidates with the most votes and decided by a [[relative majority]]. That means that the 6th ballot, in place before that, is removed.<ref name=system>{{Cite web|url=https://eretikos.gr/pos-tha-eklegetai-o-proedros-tis-dimokratias/269959|title=Πώς θα εκλέγεται ο Πρόεδρος της Δημοκρατίας|date=2019-11-25|website=eretikos.gr|language=el|accessdate=2019-12-14|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20191214214050/https://eretikos.gr/pos-tha-eklegetai-o-proedros-tis-dimokratias/269959|archivedate=2019-12-14|url-status=dead}}</ref>

==Candidates==
=== Christos Rammos ===
On 4 December 2024, the [[New Left (Greece)|New Left]] party proposed that the various left-wing parties rally behind the candidacy of the chairman of the Communications Privacy Authority (ADAE), Christos Rammos.<ref>{{Cite web|language=el|url=https://www.protothema.gr/politics/article/1570684/hristos-rammos-i-aristeri-protasi-gia-tin-proedria-i-protasi-haritsi-oi-adidraseis-famellou-kai-tsipra/|title=Χρήστος Ράμμος: Η «αριστερή πρόταση» για την Προεδρία - Η πρόταση Χαρίτση, οι αντιδράσεις Φάμελλου και Τσίπρα|website=protothema.gr|date=2024-12-04|accessdate=2024-12-27}}</ref> The [[Communist Party of Greece]] nevertheless refused to support this proposal, recalling its opposition to the existence of the presidential institution.<ref>{{Cite web|language=el|url=https://www.lifo.gr/now/politics/o-hristos-rammos-i-protasi-neas-aristeras-gia-proedro-tis-dimokratias-ohi-apo-kke|title=Ο Χρήστος Ράμμος η πρόταση Νέας Αριστεράς για Πρόεδρο της Δημοκρατίας - «Όχι» από ΚΚΕ|website=lifo.gr|date=2024-12-03|accessdate=2024-12-27}}</ref> He himself refused the nomination.<ref>{{Citation|url=https://www.tovima.gr/2024/12/16/politics/rammos-den-epithymo-na-eimai-ypopsifios-gia-proedros-tis-dimokratias/|title=Ράμμος: «Δεν επιθυμώ να είμαι υποψήφιος για ΠτΔ – Επικράτησαν οι μικροπολιτικοί υπολογισμοί και οι κομματικές στρατηγικές»|publisher=TO BHMA|date=2024-12-16}}</ref>

=== Katerina Sakellaropoulou ===
In accordance with the provisions of the [[Constitution of Greece|Constitution]], the outgoing President [[Katerina Sakellaropoulou]] is eligible for a second five-year term. She has nontheless been the subject of much criticism, notably both for her alleged inaction on the violations of the rule of law for which the conservative government has been criticized,<ref>{{Cite web|language=el|url=https://www.news247.gr/gnomes/tha-epitrepsei-i-proedros-na-ginei-koureli-to-sintagma/|title=Θα επιτρέψει η Πρόεδρος να γίνει κουρέλι το Σύνταγμα;|website=news247.gr|date=2023-12-23|accessdate=2024-11-23}}</ref> and her [[Progressivism|progressive]] stances on issues like [[LGBTQ rights|LGBT]] and [[Refugee rights|refugee]] rights,<ref>{{Cite web|language=el|url=https://www.capital.gr/arthra/3888890/gia-tin-katerina-sakellaropoulou/|title=Για την Κατερίνα Σακελλαροπούλου|website=capital.gr|date=2024-12-04|accessdate=2024-12-31}}</ref> by opposite sides of the political aisle. Moreover, during her five-year term, the presidential office suffered an unprecedented loss of popularity.<ref>{{Cite web|language=el|url=https://www.protagon.gr/epikairotita/public-issue-eksairetika-arnitiki-i-apopsi-twn-politwn-gia-tin-katerina-sakellaropoulou-44342662220|title=Εξαιρετικά αρνητική η άποψη των πολιτών για την Κατερίνα Σακελλαροπούλου|website=protagon.gr|date=2023-01-06|accessdate=2024-11-23}}.</ref><ref>{{Cite web|language=el|url=https://www.lifo.gr/stiles/daily/poy-ofeiletai-i-arnitiki-eikona-tis-katerinas-sakellaropoyloy|title=Πού οφείλεται η αρνητική εικόνα της Κατερίνας Σακελλαροπούλου;|website=lifo.gr|date=2023-01-27|accessdate=2024-11-23}}.</ref><ref>{{Cite web|language=el|url=https://www.dnews.gr/eidhseis/politikes-eidhseis/463689/i-riksi-proedrias-ekklisias-kai-oi-thesmikes-parenergeies-to-paradeigma-papoylia|title=Η ρήξη Προεδρίας - Εκκλησίας και οι θεσμικές παρενέργειες: Το παράδειγμα Παπούλια|website=dnews.gr|date=2024-03-08|accessdate=2024-11-23}}</ref>

=== Other potential candidates ===
Against this backdrop, many within the ND party called for another candidate to be put forward by the Prime Minister for this presidential election, with some party deputies going so far as to call for the appointment of a political figure chosen from the right. In this case, the names of former prime ministers [[Kostas Karamanlis]], [[Panagiotis Pikrammenos]], and [[Antonis Samaras]], as well as parliamentary speaker [[Konstantinos Tasoulas]], were mentioned.<ref>{{Cite web|language=el|url=https://www.news247.gr/magazine/politics/ziteitai-dexios-proedros-tis-dimokratias/|title=Ζητείται δεξιός Πρόεδρος της Δημοκρατίας|website=news247.gr|date=2024-06-29}}</ref> 

The possibility of a candidacy by the Minister of Culture, [[Lina Mendoni]], is also being put forward within the ranks of the parliamentary majority.<ref name="Naftemporiki">{{Cite web|language=el|title=Αυτή την Προεδρία ποιος θα την πάρει; Η λύση Σακελλαροπούλου, η έκπληξη Μενδώνη και τo τουρκικό κοστούμι|publisher=Naftemporiki|date=23 December 2024|url=https://www.naftemporiki.gr/paraskiniaka/1864030/ayti-tin-proedria-poios-tha-tin-parei-i-lysi-sakellaropoyloy-i-ekplixi-mendoni-kai-to-toyrkiko-kostoymi/|access-date=2024-12-28}}</ref>

==Results==
Parliament failed to elect a president in the first round of voting held on 25 January. Konstantinos Tasoulas received 160 votes (200 needed), Louka Katseli 40, Tassos Giannitsis 34, and Konstantinos Kyriakou 14. Since no candidate won a majority in parliament, another round of voting was held on 31 January.<ref>{{Cite web|language=en|title=Greek lawmakers fail to elect new president in first round of voting |publisher=Reuters |date=25 January 2025 |url=https://www.reuters.com/world/europe/greek-lawmakers-fail-elect-new-president-first-round-voting-2025-01-25/ |access-date=2024-12-28}}</ref> In the second round, the members voted in the same division as the first round, with 52 other members abstaining from the vote. The third round, which saw the required majority lowered to 180, was held on 6 February, which again saw little change in the voting totals.<ref>{{Cite web |date=2025-02-06 |title=Tasoulas Falls Short Again; Final Presidential Vote February 12 |url=https://www.tovima.com/uncategorized/tasoulas-falls-short-again-final-presidential-vote-set-for-february-12/ |access-date=2025-02-06 |website=tovima.com |language=en}}</ref> A final round took place on 12 February. In this round, the required majority was reduced to 151 votes. [[Konstantinos Tasoulas]] got 160 votes, thus winning the election and becoming [[President of Greece|president-elect]].<ref>{{Cite web |date=2025-02-12 |title=Νέος Πρόεδρος της Δημοκρατίας ο Κωνσταντίνος Τασούλας - Εκλέχτηκε με 160 ψήφους |url=https://www.tovima.gr/2025/02/12/politics/proedros-tis-dimokratias-o-tasoulas-eklextike-me-160-psifous/ |access-date=2025-02-12 |website=ΤΟ ΒΗΜΑ |language=el}}</ref>
