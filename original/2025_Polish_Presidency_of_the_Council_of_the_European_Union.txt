The '''2025 Polish Presidency of the Council of the European Union''' is the first in a trio of rotating [[Presidency of the Council of the European Union]] positions currently held by [[Poland]]. Poland's Presidency of the Council in 2025 is the first nation in the thirteenth trio of Council Presidencies together with [[Denmark]] and [[Cyprus]], which will last until 1 July 2026. It is Poland's second Presidency of the council, after its first in 2011.

[[Poland]] succeeded [[Hungary]] as president on <abbr>1 January</abbr> 2025. The next presidency will be held by [[Denmark]] starting on <abbr>1 July</abbr> 2025.<ref>{{Cite web |date=26 July 2016 |title=Council Decision (EU) 2016/1316 of 26 July 2016 |url=https://www.consilium.europa.eu/media/56627/presidencies-until-2030.pdf |access-date=9 July 2024 |website=Official Journal of the European Union}}</ref>

== Objectives ==
The slogan of the 2025 Polish Presidency is "Security, Europe." The objectives stated on its presidency website focus on the following priorities:<ref>{{Cite web |title=Priorities of the Polish presidency |url=https://polish-presidency.consilium.europa.eu/en/programme/priorities/ |access-date=9 July 2024 |website=Polish Presidency}}</ref>

* Stronger [[Atlanticism|trans-Atlantic]] partnership
* Border security
* Health and welfare security
* Military security
* Greater European economic and military independence
