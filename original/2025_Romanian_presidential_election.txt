Presidential elections will be held in [[Romania]] on 4 and 18 May 2025.<ref name="16Dec24">{{cite web |last=Fodor |first=Simona |date=16 December 2024 |title=Romania elections: Bucharest mayor to run for president in 2025 |url=https://www.romania-insider.com/nicusor-dan-run-president-dec-2024 |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20241217090955/https://www.romania-insider.com/nicusor-dan-run-president-dec-2024 |archive-date=17 December 2024 |access-date=22 December 2024 |website=Romania Insider}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last=Stan |first=Filip |date=2025-01-08 |title=Prima sedință a Coaliţiei de guvernare din acest an. Alegerile prezidenţiale, pe 4/18 mai 2025, Crin Antonescu rămâne candidatul Coaliţiei UPDATE |url=https://www.romaniatv.net/prima-sedinta-a-coalitiei-de-guvernare-din-acest-an-discutii-privind-data-alegerilor-prezidentiale-candidatul-comun-bugetul-pe-2025_8713183.html |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20250216144212/https://www.romaniatv.net/prima-sedinta-a-coalitiei-de-guvernare-din-acest-an-discutii-privind-data-alegerilor-prezidentiale-candidatul-comun-bugetul-pe-2025_8713183.html |archive-date=16 February 2025 |access-date=2025-01-08 |website=Romania TV |language=ro}}</ref> The election was scheduled in January 2025 following the annulment of the [[2024 Romanian presidential election|2024 presidential election]] citing [[Accusations of Russian interference in the 2024 Romanian presidential election|alleged Russian meddling]] in favour of first round winner [[Călin Georgescu]]. The campaign has been characterised by political instability and a series of major ongoing [[Accusations of Russian interference in the 2024 Romanian presidential election|protests against the annulment]]. On 7 March, Georgescu was barred from running pending several criminal investigations, with leader of the [[Alliance for the Union of Romanians|AUR]] party [[George Simion]] announcing his candidacy in Georgescu's place.

== Background ==

=== Second Ciolacu cabinet ===
After the [[2024 Romanian parliamentary election|parliamentary election]], the [[second Ciolacu cabinet]] was formed as a minority coalition between [[Social Democratic Party (Romania)|PSD]], [[National Liberal Party (Romania)|PNL]], [[Democratic Alliance of Hungarians in Romania|UDMR]] and with confidence and supply from [[Romanian ethnic minority parties|minority parties]] on 23 December 2024. Furthermore, [[Ilie Bolojan]] became [[President of the Senate of Romania]] and [[Ciprian-Constantin Șerban]] became the [[President of the Chamber of Deputies of Romania]]. The cabinet passed with a seven-vote majority and became official. Opposition leaders (most prominently [[George Simion]] and [[Party of Young People|POT]] Leader [[Anamaria Gavrilă]]) called the cabinet illegitimate and for the resignation of incumbent president [[Klaus Iohannis]].<ref>{{Cite web |date=2024-12-22 |title=Cine vor fi miniștrii din noul Guvern și cum s-au împărțit portofoliile între PSD, PNL și UDMR. === President Klaus Iohannis' resignation and political crisis ===
Following the annulment of the 2024 presidential election in December, Iohannis was allowed to stay on as president by the [[Constitutional Court of Romania|Constitutional Court]] until his successor could be sworn in. However, on 10 February, following an attempt by members of parliament to impeach him, Iohannis announced that he would resign on 12 February.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Romania's President Iohannis resigns in face of impeachment threat from hard-right opposition |url=https://www.france24.com/en/europe/20250210-romania-s-outgoing-president-resigns-in-face-of-impeachment-threat-from-opposition |access-date=10 February 2025 |website=France 24|date=10 February 2025 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20250210180356/https://www.france24.com/en/europe/20250210-romania-s-outgoing-president-resigns-in-face-of-impeachment-threat-from-opposition |archive-date=10 February 2025 |url-status=live}}</ref> Senate president [[Ilie Bolojan]] assumed the role of acting president until the elections.<ref>{{Cite web |date=12 February 2025 |title=Romania's Iohannis steps down, leaving caretaker president in charge until election rerun in spring |url=https://apnews.com/article/romania-president-europe-eu-klaus-iohannis-elections-a42440317c1ac55926789f4664656f60 |access-date=12 February 2025 |website=AP News|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20250212112811/https://apnews.com/article/romania-president-europe-eu-klaus-iohannis-elections-a42440317c1ac55926789f4664656f60|archive-date=12 February 2025|url-status=live}}</ref>

== Election date ==
The date of the first round had initially been publicly speculated by media as 23 March 2025, with the runoff two weeks later (6 April 2025). In early January, these dates became obsolete, as the law for electing the [[President of Romania]] requires a minimum of 75 days between the election day and the day the election is called. On 8 January, the coalition fixed the dates of the election. The first round is scheduled to be held on 4 May 2025 with the second round two weeks later (18 May 2025).<ref>{{Cite web |last=Garguli |first=Deniz |date=2025-01-08 |title=Guvernul a decis data noilor alegeri prezidențiale din România. == Candidates ==
The Central Electoral Bureau (BEC) was constituted on 21 February 2025.<ref>[https://prezidentiale2025.bec.ro/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/CP_1.pdf BEC 2025 press release]</ref> The deadline for registering electoral alliances at the BEC was on 23 February 2025. By that deadline, only one electoral alliance was registered, between the governing [[Social Democratic Party (Romania)|PSD]], [[National Liberal Party (Romania)|PNL]], and [[Democratic Alliance of Hungarians in Romania|UDMR]], called the Romania Forward Electoral Alliance ({{lang|ro|Alianța Electorală România Înainte}}; A.Ro for short).<ref>{{cite web|url=https://prezidentiale2025.bec.ro/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/decizie_1.pdf |title=BEC decision regarding the Romania Forward Alliance A.Ro |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20250309121453/https://prezidentiale2025.bec.ro/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/decizie_1.pdf|archive-date=9 March 2025|url-status=live}}</ref>

Persons who wished to run for the office may begin gathering the support signatures (a minimum of 200,000) after 23 February. The deadline for registering the candidacy at the BEC was 15 March 2025, at 23:59:59. All candidatures validated by the BEC must pass the scrutiny of the [[Constitutional Court of Romania]]. On 19 March, Gavrilă withdrew her candidacy for president, endorsing Simion.<ref>{{Cite news |last=Iana |first=Ciprian |date=20 March 2025 |title=Anamaria Gavrilă a anunțat oficial că se retrage din alegerile prezidențiale 2025: „Am făcut tot ce a depins de mine” |url=https://www.libertatea.ro/stiri/anamaria-gavrila-s-a-retras-din-alegerile-prezidentiale-2025-5239319 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20250320081509/https://www.libertatea.ro/stiri/anamaria-gavrila-s-a-retras-din-alegerile-prezidentiale-2025-5239319 |archive-date=20 March 2025 |access-date=20 March 2025 |work=[[Libertatea]]}}</ref> A final list of all 11 candidates was released on the following day.<ref>{{Cite news |last=Arambescu |first=Dana |date=20 March 2025 |title=Lista finală de candidați la alegerile prezidențiale 2025. Cine sunt cei 11 români care vor să ajungă la Cotroceni |url=https://www.libertatea.ro/stiri/lista-finala-candidati-alegeri-prezidentiale-2025-5239359 |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20250320092245/https://www.libertatea.ro/stiri/lista-finala-candidati-alegeri-prezidentiale-2025-5239359 |archive-date=20 March 2025 |access-date=20 March 2025 |work=[[Libertatea]]}}</ref> On 22 March, a random draw took place, placing Simion on the [[Ballot order effect|first position]] on the ballot and [[Nicușor Dan]] on the last.<ref>{{Cite news |last=Arambescu |first=Dana |date=23 March 2025 |title=Ordinea candidaților pe buletinul de vot la alegerile prezidențiale din 4 mai 2025 |url=https://www.libertatea.ro/stiri/ordinea-candidatilor-buletinul-de-vot-alegerile-prezidentiale-2025-5242788 |url-status=live |work=[[Libertatea]]}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |title=Specimen ballot paper |url=https://prezidentiale2025.bec.ro/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/Specimen_TurI_tara.pdf |website=prezidentiale2025.bec.ro}}</ref>
