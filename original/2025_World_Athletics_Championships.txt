The '''2025 World Athletics Championships''' ([[Japanese language|Japanese]]: 2025年世界陸上競技選手権大会) will be the twentieth edition of the [[World Athletics Championships]], and are scheduled to be held from 13 to 21 September 2025 in [[Tokyo]],<ref>{{cite web | url=https://worldathletics.org/news/press-releases/decisions-229-council-meeting-rome-2022 | title=Decisions made at World Athletics Council Meeting in Rome &#124; PRESS-RELEASES &#124; World Athletics | access-date=November 30, 2022 | archive-date=November 30, 2022 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20221130212305/https://worldathletics.org/news/press-releases/decisions-229-council-meeting-rome-2022 | url-status=live }}</ref> Japan. The championships will use the [[Japan National Stadium|National Stadium]], rebuilt for the Tokyo 2020 [[2020 Summer Olympics|Olympic]] and [[2020 Summer Paralympics|2020 Summer Paralympic]] Games.<ref name=":0" />

This is the third time that the championships will be held in Japan, following the [[2007 World Championships in Athletics|2007]] event in [[Osaka]], and the [[1991 World Championships in Athletics|1991]] event in Tokyo at the [[National Stadium (Tokyo)|former National Stadium]].<ref name=":0">{{cite web|url=https://www.worldathletics.org/news/press-releases/tokyo-2025-world-athletics-championships|title=Tokyo to host 2025 World Athletics Championships|date=14 July 2022|publisher=World Athletics|access-date=July 14, 2022|archive-date=December 8, 2022|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20221208190341/https://www.worldathletics.org/news/press-releases/tokyo-2025-world-athletics-championships|url-status=live}}</ref>

== Qualification ==
[[World Athletics]] approved the timetable and qualification system for the 2025 World Athletics Championships on 31 July 2024.<ref>{{cite web |date=31 July 2024 |title=Timetable and qualification system approved for WCH Tokyo 25 and WIC Nanjing 25|url=https://worldathletics.org/news/press-releases/timetable-qualification-system-world-championships-tokyo-25-indoor-nanjing-25 |accessdate=15 August 2024 |publisher=[[World Athletics]] |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240731190945/https://worldathletics.org/news/press-releases/timetable-qualification-system-world-championships-tokyo-25-indoor-nanjing-25|archive-date=31 July 2024|url-status=live}}</ref>

=== Window ===
The qualifying window for the marathon and 35 km race walk is 5 November 2023 until 4 May 2025. For the 10,000 m, 20 km race walk, combined events and relays, the window runs from 25 February 2024 to 24 August 2025. For all other events, entry standards can be achieved from 1 August 2024 to 24 August 2025.

=== Entry standards ===
The qualification system for the championships will be based on a combination of entry standards and world rankings. The entry standards<ref>{{cite web |date=31 July 2024 |title=World Athletics Championships Tokyo 25 - Target number of athletes / teams by event|url=https://assets.aws.worldathletics.org/document/66aa789d5e6a655f85e64212.pdf |accessdate=15 August 2024 |publisher=[[World Athletics]]|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20240801000541/https://assets.aws.worldathletics.org/document/66aa789d5e6a655f85e64212.pdf|archive-date=1 August 2024|url-status=live}}</ref> have been set with the aim of qualifying about 50% of the athletes.

The top five finishers at Platinum Label marathons and winners of individual events at area championships (except for the marathon), regardless of marks, will be considered as having achieved the entry standard.

The top three athletes in the [[2024–25 World Athletics Cross Country Tour]] – not qualified through entry standards or other ways – are deemed to be qualified for the 10,000 m.

The [[2025 World Athletics Relays]] scheduled for [[Guangzhou]],[[China]] will be used as a qualifying event for the relays with the top 14 teams in each event securing their place at the World Championships.
